Example ADR v4 Calculation

This example illustrates how ADR v4 is produced.

Step 1 — Base Relevance Score

Local plumber in a 30-mile radius.
Matches core query intent for “emergency plumber near me.”

Relevance: 82

Step 2 — Impact Score

Strong citation footprint

Consistent listings

4.7 review average

124 reviews across multiple platforms

Good schema alignment

Impact: 78

Step 3 — Novelty Score

Business stands out because it specializes in:

water heaters

drain clearing

emergency service

Competitors are generalists.

Novelty: 72

Step 4 — Forecast Confidence

Identity consistent

Category consistent

No major mismatches

Forecast Confidence: 85

Step 5 — Velocity Multiplier

Two new reviews and a GBP update in the last 24 hours.

Velocity Window: 18–72 hours
Multiplier applied: 1.15×

Step 6 — Contradiction Penalty

One old directory listing shows “HVAC.”

Penalty: −8

Step 7 — Final ADR Score

Base score:

(82 + 78 + 72 + 85) / 4 = 79.25


Apply velocity:

79.25 × 1.15 = 91.1


Apply penalty:

91.1 − 8 = 83.1

Final ADR v4: 83

Strong visibility.
